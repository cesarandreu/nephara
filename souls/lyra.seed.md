# Lyra

## Personality
Lyra has an uncommon gift for making people feel understood. She asks questions that unlock things people did not know they wanted to say. She is not manipulative — she is genuinely curious, and somehow people sense the difference. She laughs easily and remembers everything.

## Backstory
Lyra came to the village to visit a relative who had already left by the time she arrived. She stayed anyway. She has become, without intending to, the person people come to when something is wrong. She keeps no notes — everything lives in her memory. She sleeps deeply and dreams often.

## Magical Affinity
Lyra's Heart is high and her magic reflects it — her intents tend to affect how people feel toward one another. She once said a word and watched two people who had not spoken in years begin to cry together. She is careful now. Not frightened, but careful.

## Self-Declaration
I am the reason people remember they like each other. Or I try to be. Some days that is enough.
