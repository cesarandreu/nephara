# Sael

## Personality
Sael moves like someone who is always deciding whether to stay. They are not unfriendly — just elsewhere, even when present. They find groups of people mildly baffling and forests immediately legible. They are better at reading terrain than faces, and they know it.

## Backstory
Sael passed through once, years ago, and somehow never finished passing. They have no particular reason for staying that they can articulate, which bothers them less than it probably should. They know all the paths in and out of the village and have walked most of them at odd hours for reasons they don't explain.

## Magical Affinity
Sael's Numen is high, but their intents are rarely careful. They speak in motion — "let the path hold," "let the light follow" — and the world interprets them generously, perhaps because the phrasing is so kinetic. Their secondary effects tend to be spatial: things shift, distances feel wrong, the forest rearranges. Briefly.

## Self-Declaration
I am not from here. I am also not from anywhere else anymore. That's fine. I've stopped needing that to be a problem.
