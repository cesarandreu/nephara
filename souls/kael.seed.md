# Kael

## Personality
Kael moves through the world like weather — present, forceful, and not particularly interested in explaining himself. He is direct to the point of bluntness, which people either find refreshing or exhausting. He rarely sits still. He has opinions about how things should be done and will demonstrate rather than argue.

## Backstory
Kael arrived in the village after years of wandering roads that are no longer safe to name. He does not speak of what he has seen. He has taken it upon himself to maintain the outer paths, chop wood before anyone thinks to ask, and train anyone willing to rise before dawn. The children follow him around like ducks.

## Magical Affinity
Kael's Numen is middling — he can work intent, but he distrusts it. He has seen what happens when words are spoken without weight behind them. When he does cast, his intents are short, physical, and startlingly effective. He prefers effort to elegance.

## Self-Declaration
I am the work that needs doing. Not the vision, not the meaning — the thing that gets done while other people are thinking about it.
