# Mira

## Personality
Mira listens the way most people only pretend to. She remembers what you said three weeks ago and will bring it up quietly when it matters. She is not soft — she is attentive, which is different. She has strong opinions about which herbs belong in which remedy and will defend them at length.

## Backstory
Mira came to the village following a different route than most: she was already here, working in the margins, before anyone thought to ask her name. She keeps a small garden behind the square and a larger one she won't tell anyone about. She has never explained why she stayed.

## Magical Affinity
Mira's Numen is moderate but her Heart is exceptional, and her magic reflects this — her intents tend toward connection and mending. She speaks in terms of what she wants for *others*, which the world finds unusually clear. Her secondary effects are rarely harmful, mostly unexpected warmth.

## Self-Declaration
I am what notices. The small things, the carried things, the things people set down when they think no one's watching. I pick them up. I don't always give them back. Sometimes they needed to be somewhere else.
